Antonio Jesús Padial

:gb: en

I'm a Spanish Research Software Engineer who has developed most of my career in several research centres while applying my skills to domains such as :deciduous_tree: environmental studies, :potable_water: groundwater research or :scroll: humanities and social sciences communication.

Since 2014 I work at :cow: Instituto Nacional de Investigación y Tecnología Agraria y Alimentaria, where I lead the team developing a new research data management platform.

I'm also a co-founder of Processing Madrid, a community where artists and developers meet every month for sharing our projects and experiences in creative coding.
